# GB Medix AI SEO Foundation Plan

## 1. Homepage SEO Metadata

Primary page:

- URL: `/`
- SEO title: `AI Health Assessment | GB Medix AI`
- Meta description: `Take a free 3-minute AI health assessment, understand your body patterns, and unlock personalized wellness guidance with GB Medix AI.`
- Canonical: `/`
- Open Graph image: `/assets/medical-body-scan.png`

Primary keywords:

- AI health assessment
- AI health management
- free health assessment
- TCM constitution analysis
- personalized wellness guidance
- AI wellness report
- health score

Search intent:

- Users looking for a quick health self-check.
- Users curious about fatigue, sleep, stress, diet, and body pattern insights.
- Users comparing AI wellness tools before paying for a deeper report.
