## FAQ

### What do I get for $9.99?

You get a Premium AI Health Management Report for your completed assessment, including deeper analysis, lifestyle guidance, follow-up planning, and wellness product suggestions.

### Is this a subscription?

No. Sprint 1B Premium report access is a one-time unlock for one assessment.

### Can one payment unlock multiple reports?

No. Premium access is scoped to the assessment connected to the payment.

### What if payment succeeds but my report is still locked?

Refresh the report page after a moment. If access remains locked, support can verify the payment and entitlement state.

### What happens after a refund?

If a refund is completed, Premium access for that report is removed.

### Is the Premium report urgent-care support?

No. It is for wellness education and lifestyle planning. For urgent or serious concerns, contact local emergency services or a qualified professional.
