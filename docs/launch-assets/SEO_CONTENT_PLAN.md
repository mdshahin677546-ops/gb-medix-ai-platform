## On-Page SEO Template

Each blog post should include:

- SEO title under 60 characters.
- Meta description under 155 characters.
- H1 with primary keyword.
- Short intro answering search intent.
- 3 to 5 clear H2 sections.
- Friendly disclaimer.
- CTA to `/en/tcm-check`.

## Internal Linking

Recommended links:

- Homepage to `/en/tcm-check`.
- Blog posts to health assessment.
- FAQ to checkout/report support sections.
- Premium copy to report and checkout flow.

## Compliance Rules

- Use wellness and health management language.
- Avoid cure, emergency, or certainty claims.
- Do not imply the tool replaces a qualified professional.
- Do not promise outcomes.
