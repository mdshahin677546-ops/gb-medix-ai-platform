## FAQ

### What is GB Medix AI?

GB Medix AI is an AI Health Assessment and Health Management platform. It helps users organize everyday wellness signals and receive lifestyle guidance.

### How long does the assessment take?

Most users can complete the free assessment in about 3 minutes.

### What do I get for free?

The free result includes a health score, a constitution pattern, basic insights, and limited recommendations.

### What is included in Premium?

Premium includes deeper analysis, expanded lifestyle guidance, follow-up planning, and wellness product suggestions.

### Is this for urgent situations?

No. If something feels urgent or serious, contact local emergency services or a qualified professional.

### Can I get a refund?

Refund handling follows the payment provider process. If a refund is completed, Premium access for that report is removed.
