# Beta User Test Plan

## Goal

Validate the first commercial loop before wider launch:

```text
Landing
  ↓
Account
  ↓
Health assessment
  ↓
Free report
  ↓
Premium upgrade
  ↓
Payment
  ↓
Premium report
```

Collect:

- Experience issues.
- Payment willingness.
- Report value.
- Conversion blockers.
- Confusing language.

## Test Users

Recruit 10 testers:

- 3 wellness-curious users.
- 2 busy professionals.
- 2 users interested in TCM-inspired wellness.
- 2 users skeptical of AI health tools.
- 1 operations/support reviewer.

## Test Flow 1: First Impression

Task:

- Open Landing page.
- Explain what the product does in one sentence.

Observe:

- Do they understand the value?
- Do they trust the CTA?
- Do they notice the wellness positioning?

Questions:

- What do you think this product helps with?
- Would you click Start Free Health Assessment?
- What feels unclear?

## Test Flow 2: Registration

Task:

- Create an account.
- Complete verification if available.

Observe:

- Friction in account creation.
- Email delivery issues.
- Confusion around why an account is needed.

Questions:

- Was registration expected?
- Did you understand why account access matters?

## Test Flow 3: Health Assessment

Task:

- Complete the assessment form.

Observe:

- Questions that feel unclear.
- Form length.
- Upload section confusion.

Questions:

- Were any questions hard to answer?
- Did the assessment feel too short, too long, or right?

## Test Flow 4: Free Report Value

Task:

- Read the free report.

Observe:

- Whether the user sees immediate value.
- Whether score and constitution pattern are understandable.
- Whether recommendations feel useful.

Questions:

- What was the most useful part?
- What felt generic?
- What would make this more valuable?

## Test Flow 5: Premium CTA

Task:

- Review the Premium upgrade section.

Observe:

- Whether Premium value is clear.
- Whether price feels reasonable.
- Whether disclaimer reduces or increases trust.

Questions:

- Would you pay $9.99 for the full report?
- What would you expect to receive?
- What would stop you from upgrading?

## Test Flow 6: Checkout

Task:

- Start checkout in test mode.

Observe:

- Any confusion around Stripe page.
- Whether product name and price are clear.
- Whether redirect back to app feels smooth.

Questions:

- Did checkout feel trustworthy?
- Was the product name clear?
- Did you know what happens after payment?

## Test Flow 7: Premium Report

Task:

- Unlock and view Premium report.

Observe:

- Whether full report feels worth the price.
- Which sections are most useful.
- Whether product suggestions feel relevant.

Questions:

- Did Premium meet your expectation?
- Which section felt most valuable?
- Which section would you improve?

## Test Flow 8: Refund Expectation

Task:

- Read refund explanation.

Observe:

- Whether users understand Premium access is removed after refund.

Questions:

- Is the refund explanation clear?
- Does it feel fair?

## Test Flow 9: Mobile Experience

Task:

- Repeat Landing, assessment, and report view on mobile.

Observe:

- Text fit.
- CTA visibility.
- Form usability.
- Report readability.

Questions:

- Was anything hard to tap or read?
- Where did you feel friction?

## Test Flow 10: Support Scenario

Task:

- Imagine payment succeeded but Premium is locked.
- Find what you would do next.

Observe:

- Whether users know to refresh, wait, or contact support.
- Whether success page provides enough guidance.

Questions:

- What would you do if your report stayed locked?
- What message would reassure you?

## Scoring Rubric

Ask each tester to rate 1 to 5:

- Landing clarity.
- Trust.
- Assessment ease.
- Free report value.
- Premium value.
- Willingness to pay.
- Checkout confidence.
- Premium report usefulness.

## Key Metrics

- Landing to assessment start.
- Assessment completion.
- Free report satisfaction.
- Premium CTA click intent.
- Willingness to pay at $9.99.
- Checkout completion confidence.
- Premium perceived value.
